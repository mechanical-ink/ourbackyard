Ourbackyard

connecting and empowering communities

Welcome to Ourbackyard. Thank you for stopping by.

Ourbackyard is a project that aims to empower local communities by connecting local businesses with the people living in their community. We are often unaware of the wealth of businesses and services in our own backyard. We aim to change that by making it quick and easy to find what you need.

We are still building Ourbackyard and getting our ducks in a row. But, while we tinker away, you are invited to sign-up and show your interest in the project.

You can either sign up as a user or a business. Everyone is welcome.
